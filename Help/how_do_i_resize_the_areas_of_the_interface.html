How do I resize the areas of the interface?


LvS lets you resize the four areas of the interface: the multimedia player area, the subtitle grid, the document viewer area and the notes area.

If you place the cursor on the line dividing the interface vertically you will see that it changes shape. You may now drag it left or right, depending on which area of the interface you want to resize.


You may also resize the interface area horizontally.

Also, if you double click on the cursor at that point the area will cover the rest of the window. You have to double click it again to restore it to its initial size.

Tip: If you have accidentally resized the interface areas and are not able to restore them, just close the activity and open it again.
