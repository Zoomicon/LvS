How do I remove a per subtitle comment?


1. Click on the cell in the comment column of the subtitle whose comment you want to remove.

2. Select the white box (the first icon).

3. Press Enter.

A dialogue box will appear asking if you want to remove the specific comment and the text that it contains.


If you click OK the comment is removed from the comments area.
