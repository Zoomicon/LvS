Notes and Comments area

Comments are a way of providing feedback within the activity or draw the attention of the student at specific subtitles.

They are divided into general notes and per subtitle comments.

Also, there are Teacher notes and Student notes.

NB: In order to add a teacher note, you have to be in "Authoring mode" (Options > authoring mode).
